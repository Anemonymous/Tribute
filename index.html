Geraldine Roman

Congresswoman Geraldine Roman, one of the 100 Leading Global Thinkers of 2016 by US-based Foreign Policy magazine; one of "13 Inspiring Women of 2016" list by Time magazine

Geraldine Roman, born in 1967, was the second of four children born into the family of politicians Herminia Roman and Antonino Roman, Jr. She spent her early childhood in Orani, Bataan. She was teased by her classmates but her father taught her to be confident. She is the first ever transgender person to run for public office in the Philippines.
